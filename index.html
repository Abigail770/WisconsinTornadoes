About the Map

This map was created by Abigail Gleason for her independent practicum project class at UW-Madison in the GIS and Web Map Programming Master's degree program.

Data Sources

Tornado data: NOAA GIS

Tornadoes are among the least understood storm events in terms of climate change impacts. Over the past few decades, there has been no identifiable increase in the number of high magnitude tornadoes in the US. However, tornado events are increasingly clustered and the number of days with tornado events is increasing. This could be an effect of climate change.
